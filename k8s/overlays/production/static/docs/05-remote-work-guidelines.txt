Title: Remote Work Guidelines
Source: HR Department

TechCorp Remote Work Policy

Eligibility:
- All employees after completing 90-day probation period
- Some roles require occasional on-site presence for team meetings or client visits

Work Hours:
- Core hours: 10am to 3pm in your local timezone for meetings and collaboration
- Flexible scheduling outside core hours
- Respond to messages within 4 hours during work hours

Communication:
- Keep Slack status updated
- Use video for team meetings when possible
- Document decisions in writing on Confluence or Jira
- Weekly 1:1 with manager required

Home Office Setup:
- Reliable internet (minimum 50 Mbps recommended)
- Quiet workspace for calls
- Company provides VPN access and security software
- Equipment allowance: $500 for ergonomic setup

Performance:
- Same expectations as on-site work
- Regular check-ins with manager
- OKRs reviewed quarterly
- Focus on output and results, not hours logged

Time Off:
- Same PTO policy as on-site employees
- Notify team 24 hours in advance for planned absences
- Update calendar and set Slack status

Security:
- Use company VPN for all work
- Lock screen when away
- No work on public WiFi without VPN
- Report security concerns immediately
