Title: Customer Support Procedures
Source: Support Team

TechCorp Support Handbook

Response Times:
- Priority 1 (system down): 15 minutes
- Priority 2 (major feature broken): 1 hour
- Priority 3 (minor issues): 4 hours
- Priority 4 (questions, feature requests): 24 hours

Ticket Workflow:
1. New tickets auto-assigned based on category
2. First response acknowledges issue and sets expectations
3. Update ticket every 4 hours for P1/P2
4. Close tickets within 24 hours of resolution confirmation

Escalation Path:
- Level 1: Common issues using knowledge base
- Level 2: Technical investigation
- Engineering: Bugs requiring code changes
- Management: Customer satisfaction issues

Communication:
- Use templates for common responses
- Personalize with customer name and specific details
- Never blame the customer
- Offer workarounds when possible
- Follow up after resolution to ensure satisfaction

Tools:
- Zendesk for ticket management
- Slack #support-escalations for urgent issues
- Confluence for internal knowledge base
- Loom for video explanations

Metrics:
- Target CSAT score: 4.5/5
- First response time under SLA: 95%
- Resolution time tracked weekly
