Title: Company Benefits Overview
Source: HR Department

TechCorp Benefits Package 2024

Health Insurance:
- Choice of three plans: Basic, Standard, Premium
- Company covers 90% of employee premium, 70% for dependents
- Includes medical, dental, and vision
- HSA eligible plans available
- Company HSA contribution: $1,000 annually

Retirement:
- 401k plan with company match up to 4% of salary
- Immediate vesting
- Access to financial advisors quarterly

Time Off:
- Unlimited PTO (minimum 15 days encouraged)
- 10 paid holidays
- 12 weeks parental leave for all parents
- 5 days bereavement leave

Wellness:
- Monthly wellness stipend: $100
- Covers gym, fitness apps, mental health services
- Free access to Headspace and Calm
- Annual health screening covered

Professional Development:
- $2,000 annual learning budget
- Courses, conferences, books
- Internal tech talks weekly
- Mentorship program available

Other Perks:
- Home office stipend: $500
- Monthly team events budget
- Free lunch Fridays in office
- Commuter benefits (pre-tax)
- Employee referral bonus: $5,000
