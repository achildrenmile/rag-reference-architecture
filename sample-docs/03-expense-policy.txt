Title: Expense Reimbursement Policy
Source: Finance Department

TechCorp Expense Policy

Travel:
- Economy class flights for trips under 6 hours
- Business class allowed for international flights over 6 hours
- Hotel limit: $200/night in major cities, $150 elsewhere
- Per diem for meals: $75/day
- Book through Concur or get pre-approval for exceptions

Meals and Entertainment:
- Team lunches up to $25 per person
- Client dinners require manager approval for amounts over $100/person
- Always include attendee names and business purpose

Equipment:
- Home office equipment up to $500 one-time
- Ergonomic accessories require HR approval
- Software subscriptions must go through IT for security review

Submission Process:
1. Submit expenses within 30 days via Concur
2. Include itemized receipts for all purchases over $25
3. Manager approval required within 5 business days
4. Reimbursement processed bi-weekly on pay dates

Questions? Contact finance@techcorp.example or check the #finance channel on Slack.
